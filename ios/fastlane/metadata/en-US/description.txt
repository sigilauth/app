Sigil Auth replaces passwords with cryptographic proof. Your device becomes your credential, secured by the same hardware that protects your bank transactions.

Every authentication is a signed challenge-response using ECDSA P-256 keys stored in your iPhone's Secure Enclave. No shared secrets. No passwords to steal. No phishing.

When you log in, your paired services send a cryptographic challenge directly to your phone via push notification. You approve with Face ID or Touch ID. Your device signs the challenge. The service verifies your signature. Access granted.

Built for teams and individuals who need strong authentication without vendor lock-in. Open source (AGPL-3.0) with standard cryptography. Works with any service that implements the protocol.

Features:
• Hardware-backed ECDSA P-256 keypairs (Secure Enclave)
• Push-based authentication approval
• Multi-party authorization for sensitive actions
• Mnemonic backup for device recovery
• No vendor lock-in — standard crypto, open protocol
• Privacy-first — your keys never leave your device

Sigil Auth is open source software from Wagmi Labs. Anyone can audit the code, run their own infrastructure, or build compatible clients.
